ENTER COUNTS OF MARKS AND CONNECTORS








































The marks and connectors listed here are assumed to be all that will ever be needed by any class of signs or conceivable distance. Because the different orientations are considered separately it may seem a lot, but it quickly becomes intuitive.


It is not difficult to count marks and connectors correctly as long as you pay attention to the connectors. For example, do not miss the 3Ts, 4Ts, or corners.

A 3-TOUCH is a point where three different marks touch in some way. There is no little picture shown because this can happen in several different ways. Also for a 4-TOUCH.

The UNKNOWN on the lower right here is used to count things that are neither a recognized mark or a recognized connector. They do occur but very rarely.

TO GO BACK JUST CLICK ON THE UPPER LEFT CORNER ARROW. YOUR CURRENT COUNTS WILL STILL BE THERE.

TO LOOK AT THE ENTIRE TABLE LEAVE ALL THE COUNTS ZERO AND SET MAXDT EQUAL TO A LARGE NUMBER, SAY 50. THE DISTANCE SHOWN BETWEEN THE ZERO tvector AND EACH nzxvector IS THE SIZE OF THAT nzxvector.
